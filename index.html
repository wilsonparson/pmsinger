Biography

My research topics include state health policy, comparative politics, vulnerable populations, political communication, and health reform. My work has been published in the New England Journal of Medicine, Health Affairs, JAMA, the Journal of Health Politics, Policy and Law, and the American Journal of Public Health, amongst others. My dissertation explores the politics of Medicaid expansion, using a mixed-methods approach. I have employed varied methodologies in my work, including key-informant interviews, process tracing, and econometric techniques.
